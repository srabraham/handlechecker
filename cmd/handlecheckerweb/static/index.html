The server also checks every proposed handle for various profane terms — including ones spelled across camelCase boundaries or disguised phonetically — so you don't need to seed those yourself.

Then, propose new handles

One line per person: their first-pick handle, then any backups, separated by commas or tabs (pasted CSV or spreadsheet rows work as-is). Handles are reviewed one at a time; when one is rejected, that person's next backup is checked before moving on to the next person. A plain one-per-line list works too.
